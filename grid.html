About Us

Here at WeEat, we understand the challenges that individuals with food allergies face every day. Feeling excluded from social gatherings, limited dining options, and constant concern about ingredients can make enjoying meals a daunting task. That's why we're dedicated to creating a solution that empowers individuals with food allergies to feel included and confident in their dining experiences

How It Works

Step 1: Create an Account

Log on to our website, and create your account, selecting your food restrictions, tolerances and dietary needs

Step 2: Search

In the search bar, type your ZIP code and specify how far away you'd like to search

Step 3: Save & Eat

Scroll through your results which consist of custom tailored menus, for you and you only!
